Western University Trip 2024

February 12, 2024
UTM Physics Club visit
The UTM Physics Club visited Western University in London, Ontario, on February 9, 2024. It was a fantastic trip. I enjoyed meeting the faculty and students and learned a few things about grad school. The highlight was the Hume Cronyn Memorial Observatory. We had the opportunity to see Jupiter through the telescope, which was surreal.
